Technical Skills

In my Rutgers University certificate program, I learned a variety of skills and technologies, including the following:

Python & Pandas

Almost all of my projects have incorporated Python in some way. Many of my projects involved the use of Pandas to enhance data cleaning and analysis. I also used Jupyter Notebook to easily display dataframes and visualizations from the projects. Other libraries included Matplotlib and Plotly Express.

Tableau

I have used Tableau Public to create visualizations to tell stories about data. From the interactive visualizations, I was able to analyze the data by applying different filters; and created tableau stories from worksheets and dashboards.

R

In R, I performed statistical analyses on data. Using tidyverse and its ggplot2 library, I have cleaned datasets and displayed visualizations such as heatmaps and boxplots. Performed hypotheses testing through t-tests, anova tests, and chi-squared tests. Created multiple linear regression models to evaluate data.

SQL

I used PostgreSQL via pgAdmin to store and transform data. To organize tables, I created ERD's to map out the variables and relationships for the different tables. Imported data into tables and used different joins to export the data for further analysis.

and More
